About Melissa Ayala Designs

About Melissa Ayala Designs Melissa Ayala Designs is a Los Angeles based interior design studio specializing in residential design.

Melissa obtained a degree in interior design from The Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandising - Los Angeles while simultaneously working full time as a design assistant. Upon graduating Melissa sought out freelance opportunities to gain hands on experience in the design industry. Throughout the years Melissa has had the opportunity of designing throughout Southern California and abroad. Melissa takes pride in understanding the core of the interior design business.

“From an early age I thrived on creativity and organization. My bedroom was my sanctuary where I had creative freedom to design a space that was both beautiful and functional. They say left-brain dominant people tend to be creative and right-brain dominant people tend to be analytical, I feel like interior design is a balance of the two. I strive to thoughtfully create and execute beautiful spaces for my clients” - Melissa Ayala
